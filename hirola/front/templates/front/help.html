  • arrow_drop_downHow long does it take to ship phones?

  • arrow_drop_downWhat is a mint condition phone?
    A mint condition product is an item that has been gently used. A mint condition item differentiates itself from other quality grading systems for its three key characteristics:

    2. Perfect screen condition,
    3. No signs of use.
    Usually, our products look new, come in original packaging, are unlocked and tested to make sure they meet all the above standards.

  • arrow_drop_downAre your iPhones unlocked?
    All our products are factory unlocked, which means that they are originally sold as unlocked. This is different from carrier unlocked phones, which are originally made to work with one carrier, e.g. Safaricom, but are then unlocked to work with other carriers. We only sell factory unlocked products because we can guarantee and verify that they are compatible with all Kenyan networks.
  • arrow_drop_downDo you sell laptops?
    Not yet. Currently, we only sell tablets, phones and some home tech. Sign up for our newsletter and we can let you know when we add new stuff to our inventory.
  • arrow_drop_downHow can I order a phone not listed on the site?
    It’s simple. Contact us and ask us to find it for you. If the product meets the high standards we have set for our items, we will add it to our inventory.
teke Payments Policy
  • arrow_drop_downHow do I pay for an item on teke website?
    Currently, we operate on a Cash on Delivery system. You can place an order on teke, then we get the order ready for you. You only pay for your order once we ship it to you. We accept M-Pesa, Equitel and Z-Cash.
  • arrow_drop_downWhat if I placed an order and I no longer need the item?
    Incase you no longer need an item that you ordered, we ask that you contact us using the Help center and let us know immediately. As a courtesy to us, we request that you update us of any changes within 5 days of placing an order.
  • arrow_drop_downAre there hidden costs when I order on teke?
    There are no hidden costs when you place an order on teke. The price listed at checkout is what you pay when an order is shipped to you – nothing more, nothing less.
