How can we help you?

Tekesquared has a fantastic support. Our customer support team is always ready to answer your questions and make sure our gadgets meet the highest quality standards.

Commonly Asked questions
  • keyboard_arrow_right How long does it take to ship phones?
    It depends. Sometimes, if we have the item in our store, we ship it within 24 hours. Otherwise, we have to order items from the USA and delivery typically takes 17 business days. Despite this, we make buying from Tekesquared such a great experience by providing constant updates to let you know about the shipping progress.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightWhat is a mint condition device?
    A mint condition product is an item that has been gently used. A mint condition item differentiates itself from other quality grading systems for its three key characteristics:
    1. Functions flawlessly,
    2. Has perfect screen condition and
    3. Shows no signs of use.
    Usually, our items look new, are unlocked and come in original packaging. We test them to make sure they meet all the above standards.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightWhen will you begin shipping to other parts of Kenya?
    Unfortunately, we can only ship within Nairobi. However, we are working hard to make sure that we can deliver to other parts of the country soon.

  • keyboard_arrow_rightAre your iPhones unlocked?
    All our products are factory unlocked, which means that they are originally sold as unlocked. This is different from carrier unlocked phones, which are originally made to work with one carrier, e.g. Safaricom, but are then unlocked to work with other carriers. We only sell factory unlocked tech products because we can guarantee and verify that they are compatible with all Kenyan networks.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightDo you sell laptops?
    Yes. Currently, we mostly sell tablets, phones and some home tech devices, and a few laptops. As we grow, we aim to add more laptop products to our inventory.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightHow can I order a phone not listed on the site?
    It’s simple. Contact Us and ask us to find it for you. If the item meets the high standards we have set for our products, we will add it to our inventory.
teke Payments Policy
  • keyboard_arrow_rightHow do I pay for an item on Tekesquared website?
    Currently, we operate on a Cash on Delivery system. When you place an order on Tekesquared that's less than Kes. 25,000, we work to get the it ready for you. You only pay for your order once we ship it to you. We accept M-Pesa, Equitel and Z-Cash. If the item you order is more than kes. 25,000, you need to pay the balance first before we can process your order (e.g. if you buy an iphone 7 of Kes. 28,000, you'll need to pay Kes. 3,000 after you have ordered, then you will pay the balance upon delivery. We will email you instructions on how to pay the balance before we process your order
  • keyboard_arrow_rightWhat if I placed an order and I no longer need the item?
    Incase you no longer need an item that you had ordered, you can cancel your order within 24 hours. Just go to My profile > My Orders and press the cancel button.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightAre there hidden costs when I order on Tekesquared?
    There are no hidden costs when you place an order on Tekesquared. The price listed at checkout is what you pay when an order is shipped to you – nothing more, nothing less.

  • keyboard_arrow_rightHow do I pick up my phone after ordering?
    If you are within Nairobi area, we will deliver any order to you for free. Alternatively, you can pick up the ordered item at our offices located at Ever Green Center, Kiambu road. Unfortunately, we don’t offer deliveries to areas outside of Nairobi yet, but we hope to change that soon.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightWhat payment methods do you accept?
    Once we deliver your order, you can pay using various methods: we accept M-Pesa, cash, PayPal, and Equitel. We do not accept cash.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightWhat it the return policy?
    It’s simple: you can return any item that you buy from us in its original condition within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked. We value our customers and have faith in the quality of the products we sell, but sometimes things happen and one needs to return an item. In such a case, we make this process easy: just Contact Us and we will arrange on how you will send the item to us.
