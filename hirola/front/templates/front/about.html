About teke

Teke is an online store that sells mint condition phones to the Kenyan community

Our mission
whatshot

We believe that every Kenyan should have a quality smartphone at an affordable price. Therefore our mission is simple: We want to deliver quality smartphones for Kenyans at an affordable price

Our Story
thumb_up

For many years, there have been many low quality phone models from abroad in the market and extremely high prices of new smartphones. This has made it impossible for Kenyans to afford quality smartphones. At Teke, we are changing things for the better.

Quality assurance
verified_user

On Teke you buy quality mint condition smartphones sourced directly from the USA and delivered to you on your doorstep at an affordable price. Mint condition phones are phones that are resold with (1) perfect screen condition (2) function flawlessly and (3) have no signs of use.

No junk
phonelink_setup

To uphold our promise of providing only quality original-model phones, we handpick the phones ourselves We ensure that they are compatible with the Kenyan network providers and are free from defects. As a principle We dont sell broken/fake goods.

How it works
Its simple
  1. Login to your account
  2. Select your smartphone(s) of choice and add them to the cart.
  3. When ready, checkout, click confirm and pay
  4. We will contact you with the delivery/pickup details within 12 hrs of receiving your order.
