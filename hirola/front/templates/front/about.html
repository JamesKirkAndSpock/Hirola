About teke

Teke is an online store that sells mint condition phones to the Kenyan community

Our Mission
whatshot

We believe that every Kenyan should have a quality smartphone at an affordable price. Therefore our mission is simple: We want to deliver quality smartphones for Kenyans at an affordable price

Our Story
thumb_up

For many years, there have been many low quality phone models from abroad in the market and extremely high prices of new smartphones. This has made it impossible for Kenyans to afford quality smartphones. At Teke, we are changing things for the better.

Quality Assurance
verified_user

On teke you buy quality mint condition smartphones sourced directly from the USA and delivered to you on your doorstep at an affordable price. Mint condition phones are phones that are resold with (1) perfect screen condition (2) function flawlessly and (3) have no signs of use.

No Junk
phonelink_setup

To uphold our promise of providing only quality original-model phones, we handpick the phones ourselves We ensure that they are compatible with the Kenyan network providers and are free from defects. As a principle We dont sell broken/fake goods.

The teke Story

teke was founded in 2018 by Daniel, Winsy and Peter, long-time friends and software and mobile developers. The inspiration for teke sparked when Murage got numerous requests from people in Kenya to ship them phones from the USA. After looking over the internet for hours (and days!), he soon realized how hard it was to find unlocked phones that worked in multiple countries.

But, he also discovered that great unlocked phones existed at great prices! Talking over this with Winsy and Peter, they discovered that there was a market need for quality phones, and they had a solution. Hence, teke began as a side-project to build a better marketplace for buying quality mobile devices that can be resold to Kenya at affordable prices. Quality, integrity, simplicity and low fees made teke different then and still do to this day. From the first teke sale (a Galaxy Note 5 bought by Winsy), teke has grown to be the ultimate used tech marketplace in Kenya. We enjoy creating a platform that makes it easy for people to directly buy great devices and everything we do revolves around this concept.
