About Tekesquared

Tekesquared is an online store that sells mint condition electronics to the Kenyan community

Our Mission
whatshot

We believe that every Kenyan should have access to quality electronic devices at an affordable price. Therefore, our mission is simple: we want to make people's lives easier and better by providing access to quality technology for Kenyans at an affordable price.

Our Story
thumb_up

For many years, there have been many low quality tech products from abroad in the Kenyan market, and extremely high prices of new smartphones. This has made it impossible for Kenyans to afford quality devices. At Tekesquared, we are changing things for the better.

Quality Assurance
brightness_auto

On Tekesquared you buy quality mint condition electronics sourced directly from the USA/Canada and delivered to your doorstep at an affordable price. Mint condition phones are phones that are resold with (1) perfect screen condition (2) function flawlessly and (3) have no signs of use.

No Junk
phonelink_setup

To uphold our promise of providing only quality original-model phones, we handpick the phones ourselves and ensure that they are compatible with the Kenyan network providers and are free from defects. As a principle, we don't sell broken/fake goods.

The Tekesquared Story

Tekesquared was founded in 2018 by Murage and Peter, long-time friends and software and mobile developers.

The inspiration for Tekesquared sparked when Murage got numerous requests from people in Kenya to ship them phones from the USA. Turning to online marketplace platforms in Kenya proved unfruitful; fake phones, junk devices, inefficient communication, and downright fraud plagued the antiquated marketplaces.

Inspired to find a solution, Tekesquared began as a project to build a better marketplace for selling quality technology devices to Kenyans at affordable prices. Quality, integrity, simplicity and low fees made Tekesquared different then and still do to this day. From the first Tekesquared sale (a Galaxy Note 5), Tekesquared aims to grow to be the ultimate used tech marketplace in Kenya. We enjoy creating a platform that makes it easy for people to directly buy great devices and everything we do revolves around this concept.
